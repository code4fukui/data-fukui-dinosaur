# data-fukui-dinosaur

> 日本語のREADMEはこちらです: [README.ja.md](README.ja.md)

A dataset containing 70 photos (taken with an iPhone 6 in December 2015) documenting a visit to the **Fukui Prefectural Dinosaur Museum** in Katsuyama City. The museum is one of the three largest dinosaur museums in the world and Japan's premier paleontology institution.

## Dataset Highlights

-   **Architecture & Grounds:** The museum's iconic silver geodesic dome, outdoor dinosaur sculptures, and the massive 17.5m White-saurus (Tyrannosaurus rex) sculpture at the nearby Katsuyama Geopark.
-   **Dinosaur Exhibits:** A massive in-situ **Camarasaurus fossil**, life-size animated Tyrannosaurus rex and Stegosaurus models, and dedicated galleries for species discovered in Fukui: *Fukuisaurus*, *Fukuiraptor*, *Koshisaurus*, and *Fukuititan*.
-   **Geological & Earth History Exhibits:** A comprehensive collection of rare mineral specimens, Precambrian stromatolites, and Paleozoic fossils.
-   **Visitor Experience:** The three-floor descending spiral escalator, anime character photo boards, dinosaur-themed menu items (e.g., "Dinosaur Nest Curry") from the Dino Cafe, and exclusive souvenirs like commemorative medals.

## Dataset Structure

The dataset consists of a static collection of images.

-   `photos/2015-12/`: Contains all 70 `.jpg` image files.

No metadata files or API are provided.

## About the Museum

The Fukui Prefectural Dinosaur Museum opened in 2000. The Kitadani Formation in its home of Katsuyama City has yielded numerous important dinosaur fossils, leading to the discovery of several new species native to the prefecture.
